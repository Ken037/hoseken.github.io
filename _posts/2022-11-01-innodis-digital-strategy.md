## Overview

**Client:** Innodis  
**Brands:** Prodigal (chicken products) & DairyVale (yogurt)  
**Type:** Digital Strategy Proposal  
**Platforms:** Facebook (primary), TikTok (emerging)

This proposal represents one of the most comprehensive pieces of strategic work in my portfolio — a full digital audit, competitive benchmark, and forward-looking strategy for two of Mauritius's most recognisable food brands.

---

## The Brief

Innodis needed a clear digital roadmap for Prodigal and DairyVale: how to grow their social media presence, improve content quality, compete more effectively against well-funded rivals, and build audiences that translate into real commercial results.

---

## Competitive Landscape Analysis

Before recommending anything, I conducted a thorough analysis of the competitive landscape.

### Prodigal's Competitors (Chicken Category)

| Brand | Followers | Distinctive Strategy |

**Prodigal** sat at approximately 31,000–32,000 followers — competitive but with significant room for quality improvement.

### DairyVale's Competitors (Yogurt Category)

| Brand | Followers | Distinctive Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Yop** | ~48,000 | #LoveYopChallenge on TikTok, memes, regular contests |
| **Yoplait** | ~33,021 | "Tasting Mauritius" campaign, nutritionist content, recipe series |

A key insight emerged: the competitors leading in engagement were consistently those investing in **video content** and **audience participation mechanics**.

## Strategy for Prodigal

### The Video Imperative

Data clearly showed that video content dramatically outperformed static posts:

- **Video reach:** ~45,000 average vs ~20,000 for static images
- **Video engagement:** ~40% vs ~6% for static images

This shaped the entire content recommendation. Three recurring video formats were proposed:

| Format | Concept |
|---|---|
| **Découvercle** | Behind-the-scenes and discovery content |
| **Vite Fait! Bien Fait!** | Quick, practical cooking tips |
| **La kot-kot minute** | Bite-sized tips and tricks featuring the Kot-Kot mascot |

### Authentic Local Content

Rather than pursuing traditional influencer deals, I recommended a more authentic approach: partnering with local restaurants and chefs whose audiences are already food-engaged and who would use Prodigal products organically.

Target partners included popular local spots such as Robirosh Burgers, Taste of Mamou, and Cozy Burger — brands with genuine community credibility.

### The "Best Cook in Your Region" Campaign

A community-building competition concept designed to generate user-generated content, increase regional engagement, and create a pipeline of authentic testimonial content — with the most-mentioned participants featured in dedicated posts.

### Mascot Strategy

Kot-Kot, Prodigal's existing mascot, was underutilised. I recommended significantly increasing Kot-Kot's visibility across community events, social campaigns, and branded content to build emotional brand equity — taking inspiration from Chantefrais's highly effective "Chantal" mascot strategy.

## Strategy for DairyVale

**Brand Positioning Recommended:** *"Le plaisir de se faire du bien"* (The pleasure of doing good for yourself)

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Feel-Good** | Emotional content — happiness, peace, lifestyle moments |
| **Health & Wellness** | Mental health tie-ins, physical activity, mindfulness |

### Key Insight: Product Dilution

A critical finding was that DairyVale was posting across too many products simultaneously, diluting the impact of each. The recommendation was to adopt a **serial product focus** — running consecutive posts around a single product before moving to the next — creating a stronger impression and clearer recall for each line.

### Seasonal Campaign Concepts

- **Summer is Back** — lifestyle-led summer campaign
- **Duet for Valentines** — paired content for couples
- **Aventures de Ti-Malin** — school holiday engagement series targeting families

---

## Algorithm Recommendations (Applicable to Both Brands)

Understanding Facebook's content evaluation framework was central to the strategy:

> Facebook ranks content by: **Content Type → Video Formats → Popularity → Recency → Value Delivered**

Practical recommendations included:
- Prioritising genuine content over animation for higher reach
- Optimising posting times based on audience activity data
- Creating content designed to prompt replies and interactions
- Maintaining a consistent posting schedule to build algorithmic trust
