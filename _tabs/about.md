# Hello, I'm Hoseken

I'm a **digital marketing specialist** based in Mauritius, with a passion for building brands that genuinely connect with their audiences. From campaign strategy and content direction to influencer partnerships and social media management, I help brands find a clear, compelling voice in an increasingly noisy digital world.

Over the years I've had the privilege of working with some exciting names — from global FMCG powerhouses to beloved local Mauritian brands — crafting campaigns that are rooted in culture whilst delivering real business results.

---

## What I Do

**Social Media Strategy**
I develop platform-specific strategies across Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok that drive meaningful engagement — not just vanity metrics. Every strategy starts with understanding the audience and the algorithm.

**Content Creation & Direction**
Whether it's a product launch, a brand equity campaign, or a CSR initiative, I conceptualise and direct content that tells a story worth sharing. I work closely with photographers, videographers, and creators to bring ideas to life.

**Influencer & Creator Partnerships**
I identify and collaborate with local creators who genuinely resonate with their communities, ensuring brand messages feel authentic rather than transactional.

**Brand Strategy & Competitive Analysis**
I help brands understand where they sit within their category, benchmark against competitors, and develop a positioning narrative that sets them apart — backed by data, not just instinct.

**Event & Campaign Marketing**
From monthly content calendars to integrated multi-platform campaigns, I plan and execute communications that build anticipation, drive attendance, and keep communities engaged.

## A Bit More About Me

I grew up in Mauritius, and that background shapes everything I do. I understand the nuances of local audiences — their humour, their values, their cultural references — and I bring that intelligence to every campaign I create.

I'm equally comfortable working with global brand guidelines and adapting them sensitively to a local context, or building a brand narrative entirely from scratch for a homegrown business.

When I'm not crafting campaigns, you'll find me keeping a close eye on emerging social media trends, exploring new content formats, or simply enjoying everything the island has to offer.
